Advanced Neural
Architectures

DermoTriage is powered by state-of-the-art deep learning models purpose-built for clinical dermatology—delivering accuracy that rivals board-certified specialists.

⚡
EfficientNet-B4 Backbone

Compound-scaled convolutional network optimised for high-accuracy image classification with minimal compute — trained on 2 M+ clinical images.

🎯
Grad-CAM Explainability

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping highlights the exact skin regions driving each prediction, giving clinicians transparent, auditable insights.

📊
Confidence Thresholding

Probabilistic output calibration ensures the model only returns a diagnosis when confidence exceeds a clinically validated threshold — reducing false positives.

🔄
Multi-Task Learning

A shared encoder simultaneously classifies condition type, estimates severity, and predicts urgency — all in a single forward pass under 30 ms.

FAQ

Common questions

Is DermoTriage a replacement for a dermatologist?

No. DermoTriage is a triage tool that helps you understand the urgency of your concern and whether professional attention is needed. It does not replace clinical diagnosis or treatment.

Are my photos stored or shared?

No. Images are processed in-memory for analysis only and are never stored, sold, or shared. We are fully HIPAA-compliant.

How accurate is the AI?

Our model has been validated at 98.2% accuracy across 50+ conditions on a dataset of 2M+ clinical images. Accuracy varies by condition type and image quality.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for a one-time triage. Create a free account to save your history and track conditions over time.

Which countries is this available in?

DermoTriage is available globally. Doctor referral features are currently available in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, with more countries coming soon.
